FAQ

  1. Q: Where are some of your locations?
    A: Right now, we have worked with NYC and have set up several vendor shacks in Central Park. We have also targeted parks in other urban areas, and plan to roll out sites on popular beaches

  2. Q: What are the costs of renting a piece of equipment?
    A: We charge a varying rate per hour all of our balls and equipment:
    • Soccer ball: $5 per hour
    • Basketball: $5 per hour
    • Football: $5 per hour
    • Tennis ball: $1 per hour
    • Other miscellaneous balls: $4 per hour
    • All equipment: $10 per hour

  3. Q: What stops customers from stealing/damaging equipment?
    A: In order to rent equipment, the user must provide the shack with some form of ID to hold onto, and return once they return the equipment. If they physically damage the equipment, they must also provide a replacement fee.

  4. Q: Are there any other services/packages offered?
    A: Yes, we offer a few different modes of service:
    No package: prices are stated as above.
    Standard: 50% on all equipment at all locations ($10 a month).
    Premium: All equipment is free at all locations ($20 a month).
